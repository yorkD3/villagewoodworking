More About Us

Village Woodworking is a Fine Woodworking company serving the Greater Omaha and Lincoln Areas. Our goal is to take old and new spaces and create something uniquely "you". We work with clients to craft a design that captures the unique experience or brand of homeowners, businesses, and designers alike while adding undeniable value.

Every single project taken on excites me. When involved with Village Woodworking, you aren’t hiring some contractor that has five active jobs and is trying to get in and out as quickly as possible. You are hiring a team that genuinely cares for your space, your business, and your village. We firmly believe in providing the best service possible.

Your project will be my most important project as we only operate with one active job at a time.
