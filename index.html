Welcome to the Village

At Village Woodworking, an artistic craftsmanship is presented to the community. With custom fine furniture, specialty room, kitchen & bath design. The Village's mission is to create impact-fully timeless designs that enhance the lives of those whom we work with by highlighting their unique lived experiences. Inspired by the knowledge that "it takes a village", we are proud to not only build with excellence in mind, but to create lasting connections -- because our family is our village.
